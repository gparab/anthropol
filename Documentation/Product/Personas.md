# Use Cases & User Personas: Humanity at Scale

## 1. User Personas

### 👤 Alex: The DeFi Architect (Institutional)
- **Background:** Senior Engineer at a Top-20 Protocol.
- **Objectives:** Implement Sybil-resistance for a $100M DAO vote.
- **Pains:** Current solutions (passport scans) are too intrusive. Botnets are currently swinging votes.
- **Usage of Anthropol:** Alex integrates the SDK into the voting dashboard. Users perform a 5-second pulse check before casting a vote. The identity is stored as a non-custodial hash tied to the user's wallet.

### 👤 Sarah: The Scalp-Prevention Lead (Retail)
- **Background:** Product Manager at a major global e-commerce site.
- **Objectives:** Ensure "Drop" items (shoes, tech) go to humans, not resellers.
- **Pains:** Bot automation bypasses traditional CAPTCHAs easily.
- **Usage of Anthropol:** Sarah gates the "Buy Now" button with the Anthropol Humanity Oracle. Bots fail the temporal drift check, while humans pass seamlessly.

### 👤 Marcus: The Trust & Safety Lead (Social)
- **Background:** VP at a burgeoning social platform.
- **Objectives:** Purge botnets and disinformation campaigns.
- **Pains:** Identity verification currently costs $5/user and takes 2 days.
- **Usage of Anthropol:** Marcus uses Anthropol as a "Humanity Signal" for account verification. The cost drops by 90%, and verification is instantaneous.

## 2. Institutional Use Cases

### Case 01: Sybil-Resistant Airdrops
**Context:** A new L2 blockchain wants to distribute tokens to real users.
**Mechanism:** Users must provide an Anthropol Humanity Proof.
**Value:** Prevents a single attacker from creating 10,000 wallets to drain the treasury.

### Case 02: Healthcare Data Access
**Context:** A hospital network provides remote access to sensitive records.
**Mechanism:** Doctors use Anthropol for biological 2FA.
**Value:** Ensures the person accessing the data is the physical doctor, not a stolen session cookie.

### Case 03: Digital Town Square
**Context:** A city government implements an online petition system.
**Mechanism:** Residents sign through the Anthropol humanity gate.
**Value:** Ensures every signature represents a biological citizen, preventing synthetic astroturfing.
