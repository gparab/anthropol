# PRD: Anthropol Identity Protocol

## 1. Product Vision
To restore humanity to the digital internet by providing a friction-free, privacy-preserving layer that distinguishes biological reality from synthetic simulation.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Fintech/L1/L2 Protocols:** Managing Sybil-resistant airdrops and governance.
- **E-commerce:** Mitigating inventory depletion by scalper botnets.
- **Social Media:** Verifying "Real Human" status for verified accounts.

## 3. Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance:** Sub-1.5s end-to-end verification latency.
- **Privacy:** 0% PII storage in all database shards.
- **Scalability:** Support for 1,000+ verification requests per second per shard.
- **Resilience:** Hardware-bound identity to prevent cloud-emulator attacks.

## 4. User Personas

### A. The Institutional Developer (Alex)
- **Goal:** Integrate Sybil resistance into a new DeFi protocol in < 1 day.
- **Pain:** Doesn't want to manage biometric data or deal with GDPR liability.
- **Needs:** Clean SDKs, good documentation, and Zero-Knowledge proofs.

### B. The Compliance Officer (Sarah)
- **Goal:** Ensure the platform doesn't violate regional data laws.
- **Pain:** High cost of manual identity verification.
- **Needs:** Regional data sovereignty and automated audit reports.

### C. The End-User (Human)
- **Goal:** Access a gated community or platform.
- **Pain:** Hates CAPTCHAs and taking pictures of their passport.
- **Needs:** 5-second seamless verification without "Big Brother" feeling.
